About Us

Good Hands was founded by three passionate visionaries — Hanan, Hayat, and Husniya — with a shared dream: to create a platform where every donation makes a real difference. From humble beginnings, we started with a simple idea: to connect generous hearts with those in need, transparently and effectively. Over the years, our journey has been guided by empathy, collaboration, and the belief that small actions can create huge impact. Today, Good Hands is our way of turning that belief into reality, and we continue to strive toward making charitable giving seamless, trustworthy, and meaningful.

Hanan Seid

Co-Founder & Visionary Leader. Hanan drives our mission forward, ensuring every initiative aligns with our values and vision.

Hayat Mohammed

Co-Founder & Strategy Lead. Hayat crafts our strategy, focusing on growth, impact, and long-term sustainability.

Husniya Mohammed

Co-Founder & Community Advocate. Husniya ensures our platform remains grounded in real stories and community needs.
